## 🌿 About Us

At **Bliss Shore Ayur Care Post Natal Care Centre**, we are dedicated to nurturing mothers and newborns through the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda.

Located in the peaceful surroundings of **Thannikundungal, Valiyaparamba, Kozhikode**, our centre blends traditional healing practices with modern comfort to support complete postnatal recovery.

We believe motherhood deserves care, healing, and attention — physically, emotionally, and spiritually.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Our mission is to provide a **safe, rejuvenating, and empowering postnatal journey** by combining:

- Authentic Ayurvedic treatments  
- Personalized care for mother & baby  
- Modern accommodation & comfort  
- Expert medical supervision  

We aim to create a **healing sanctuary for bonding, recovery, and renewal**.
